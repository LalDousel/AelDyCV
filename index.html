About Me

I was born in the year 1997,september 20.It was on a small village in Lamka,named Mualkoi.The earliest memories i had of my early,childhood life was that of Nagaland,Tuensang to be specific.My dad was in the army we stayed in an army quarter ,went to army school,moved from place to place quiet a bit.Been to Mantripukhri in Imphal,Tuensang in Nagaland,Jorhat etc.We finally decided to settle in 2005 in mualkoi,the place where i was born.And the rest,to all my friends,relatives and family staying in this village,IS HISTORY.I guess that's all i can say about my early life.Want to know more?Let me know.

So let me see....,If i remember correctly i attended my nurseries in Tuensang(wrong spelling maybe,sorry),Nagaland where i did my LKG and UKG? Because my dad got transferred to Imphal,Mantripukhri to be specific,we had to move with him.I attended my classes 1 to 2,maybe..i can't remember it clearly anymore.In 2005 we moved to Mualkoi,my current place of residence where i attended classes 3 to 10 in a local school,Covenant School..such good memories!!

After completing my secondary education(Class 10) i got admitted to Rayburn High Schhol where i took science(physics,math and chemistry as my main),completed high School in 2016.

Took a year leave before i appeared for engineering entrance exam(JEE MAINS) in 2017 and tahnkfully cleared the exam. Got admitted to NIT Manipur,where i currently am pursuing B.Tech in CSE,as of making this page

I currently live in Mualkoi,and i am passionate about pursuing Web development and Game DEvelopment.I know,i have lots of interest,but hopefully i can achieve those bullseyes that i have in my mind. But let me be totally honest,i have soooo many things that i like to try,as a hobby though,like learning guitar,beatboxing,coding languages ,etc etc. I feel like i am like the Jack of ALL Trades,i have like surface level knowledge at a vast array of subjects but i am totally not good at any of them to make a career for myself.Thank You!!
